About Me

If I could be anyone in the world, who would I be? I would absolutely choose NOT to be the guy writing a bio about himself right now. Let me switch gears and talk about my dog. I’m one of those big tall guys with a tiny adorable dog. I can’t help it if I like adorable things! And lets be honest, if you don’t like adorable things, you are some kind of monster that is well beyond reproach. My dog; his name is Django and he is a miniature pinscher. He weighs 8 lbs (used to weigh 6, the little tubber), and he has quite the plethora of nicknames. Rat, ratty, ratty cakes, rattems, doodles, princess, baby boy…you get the idea. In case you missed the point, he looks like a rat. Only with long legs like a little rat-deer. And he always has a snaggle-tooth hanging out. I acquired him used, and I had to have 17 teeth pulled.
